HOW TO PLAY



To begin playing the game, press enter on your keyboard.

The goal of the game is to fight off enemies and get the highest amount of points possible.

To shoot the enemies coming at you, click on your mouse in the direction you'd like to shoot.

To move around the screen, use the left, right, up and down keys.

The score is displayed above the arena when the game is played,

and the score increases by 5 for each enemy that is killed.

Have fun playing!
